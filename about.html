Who we are

Making your safe spaces
safer and cleaner

Mr. Pierre Guillemette

As Chief Technology Officer, Pierre has a 25 year history as an inventor and entrepreneur. His experience extends from industrial-grade motor design and fabrication to power generation systems to waste-to-energy projects. After completing a diploma in Aircraft Technician Engineering, he co-founded Malette Power Capital Solutions and Malette Energy Services Inc and established Rewind Plus, a business in the electrical motor field specializing in pumps and ventilation for the mining industry. Mr. Guillemette’s projects have allowed him to specialize in infrastructure and power design solutions for renewable energy projects in the fields of wind and solar power. For the past decade he has also worked as a consultant on several large scale waste-to-energy projects in Brazil and Africa. His deep understanding of aerosol dynamics draws from his work in advanced nozzle technology and design, originally designed for chemical free water purification applications.

As the coronavirus pandemic loomed, Pierre turned his expertise in nozzle technology to address the inherent problems with bringing aerosol sterilization solutions to community workplaces. He developed an innovative and advanced nozzle design as part of a complete aero-sterilization system that removes airborne and surface pathogens from enclosed workspaces, such as office environments, schools and transportation systems. Leaning on his past work in developing countries, he designed the entire system so that it could be economical enough for developing countries to use and could be operated with minimal maintenance. Through hundreds of iterative stages, SaniCloud has evolved into the ideal system to provide an added layer of PPE to further reduce risk of viral transmission in professional offices and other indoor workspaces.

When he is not working in his lab, he can be found spending time with his wife and four children or biking one of the many trails in the Gatineau Park.

Gyneya Dicks

Gyneya Dicks leads Sanicloud Canada as Chief Operating Officer.

After completing a BCom at the University of Ottawa, Gyneya started her career at Merrill Lynch as an associate investment advisor and earned her CFA charter. She simultaneously opened several Booster Juice franchises and now owns a number of Ottawa restaurants. She completed her EMBA at Columbia Business School in 2009. She has experience applying her financial and operational expertise in M&A. Her experience in operations, finance and strategic planning are now applied to the task of bringing SaniCloud Canada's innovative solution to those organizations and professionals that most need it to continue providing their essential services during this pandemic.

As an owner of multiple restaurants, Gyneya watched as the coronavirus pandemic continued to erode her business. When Pierre Guillemette approached her about his most recent invention, an aero-sterilization device that had the ability to kill airborne and surface viruses in less than three minutes (yes, that includes COVID-19), she thought it could be a solution to having clients and staff return to the restaurants more safely. In collaboration with healthcare professionals, she continued to work with Mr. Guillemette and a team of industrial designers and engineers to refine the SaniCloud system. Through her work with medical professionals it became evident that the negative impact from the virus extended to their fields as well and limited their ability to deliver a pre-pandemic level of healthcare to patients. Thus the project grew to focus on bringing clean room technology to office environments and after four prototypes, the company has a launch ready version for professional offices.
