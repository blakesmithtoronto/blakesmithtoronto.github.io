SANICLOUD1000

The SANICLOUD1000 uses our proprietary, Aero-sanitization process to kill COVID-19 viruses in the air and on surfaces quickly and cost-effectively. The machine disperses micro-droplets of disinfectant to create a wave of Aero-sanitizer which can treat both the air and surfaces. The automated system measures and controls the air saturation and humidity within the room to ensure the appropriate amount of disinfectant is released for a complete decontamination process.

The method leverages 70 years of scientific data on best practices for sterilization of hospital and healthcare environments. The SANICLOUD system brings previously unaffordable technology to the community in a safe, effective, easy-to-use system that kills viruses, bacteria and fungi.
