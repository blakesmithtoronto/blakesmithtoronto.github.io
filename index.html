The Process

The system leverages 70 years of scientific data on best practices for sterilization of hospital and healthcare environments. The SANICLOUD system brings previously unaffordable technology to the community in a safe, effective, easy-to-use system that kills viruses, bacteria and fungi.

The machine disperses micro-droplets of disinfectant to create a wave of Aero-sanitizer which can treat both the air and surfaces. The automated system measures and controls the air saturation and humidity within the room to ensure the appropriate amount of disinfectant is released for a complete decontamination process.

Small droplets can capture particles circulating in the air and disinfect floating clusters of COVID-19.

Larger droplets provide more efficient decontamination of surfaces but are sized to avoid excessive wetness thereby preventing damage to room contents.
